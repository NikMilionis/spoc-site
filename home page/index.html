Who are SPOC?

SPOC is a student run Clark organization that serves to feed all interests affiliated with nerd culture. In accomplishing this, we host numerous events, including weekly game nights, anime showcase nights, Magic the Gathering events, creative campus events, a field trip to King Richard's (Rennaisance) Faire, and even an in-house convention!. All, of course, are welcome at SPOC and it's events, so long as you respect each other and our stuff. More detailed rules of conduct can be found online (via the club's charter) or within our office, located in UC Higgins, 3rd Floor (our door has Spock on it, so we shouldn't be too hard to find at that point).
